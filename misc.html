Languages

  • French - B2

    • (2017) When I was a freshman, I was hyped about working at Huawei after visiting their Istanbul headquarters. I continued my studies for fun. I applied for a lot of schools in China, Taiwan and Singapore, instead I was placed in a university in Korea.

    • (2019) You can probably guess "Well since I am going to Korea next year, why am I not taking up Korean?" would be my first thought after this point. I started learning Korean on January 2019. I continued my studies for a year. On February 2020, I cancelled my exchange program in Seoul, Korea since I figured I wouldn't be able to realize my plans due to the novel coronavius.

Photography

I started analog photography in my first year in college, using my grandfather's soviet made Zenit-12xp. Since then, it has turned into a nice hobby. I collect cameras of different specs when I travel abroad. I've been carrying around a camera or two on every occasion and try to make photography a part of my everyday life. I also took some Wedding & Event photography jobs to help me pay the rent when I tried living alone.
